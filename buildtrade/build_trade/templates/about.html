Our Story

Founded in 2005, BuildTrade has grown from a small local supplier to one of the leading construction material providers in the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality construction materials at competitive prices with reliable service.

Over the years, we have expanded our product range and service offerings to meet the evolving needs of the construction industry. Today, we serve a diverse clientele including contractors, builders, architects, and homeowners.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation. We continuously strive to improve our products and services to exceed our customers' expectations.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality construction materials and exceptional service that contribute to the success of our customers' projects while maintaining the highest standards of business ethics and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred supplier of construction materials, recognized for our product quality, innovation, customer service, and contribution to sustainable construction practices.

Our Values

  • Quality: We are committed to providing products that meet or exceed industry standards.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize customer satisfaction and build long-term relationships.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new and better ways to serve our customers.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally responsible business practices.
